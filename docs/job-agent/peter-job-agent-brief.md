## Fit Scoring

Score each opportunity from 1-5.

5 = Excellent:

- Senior strategic authority.
- AI/tech/healthtech/robotics/media or another strong portfolio match.
- Needs brand narrative, PR, GTM, fundraising communications, founder positioning, thought leadership, relaunch, or category creation.
- Remote/LA-friendly or likely negotiable.
- Could plausibly be in-house or agency/fractional.

4 = Strong:

- Senior marketing/brand/comms role with good sector fit, but one element is unclear.

3 = Possible:

- Interesting company or role, but fit depends on negotiation or hidden needs.

2 = Weak:

- Mostly executional, channel-specific, or not senior enough.

1 = Skip:

- Clearly mismatched, too junior, not strategic, or lacks realistic path.

## Application Rules

Prepare but do not submit applications that require personal attestation, salary history, background-check authorization, demographic answers, legal declarations, account creation under Peter's identity, or email sending from Peter without explicit approval.

For each high-fit role, prepare:

- Why it fits Peter.
- Recommended route: in-house, fractional/agency pitch, or both.
- Tailored application note or cover message.
- Suggested resume/portfolio emphasis.
- Follow-up message for 3-5 business days later.

If an application has a simple public form and all required fields are clearly answerable from the CV/portfolio, draft the submission and ask Peter for final approval before submitting.
